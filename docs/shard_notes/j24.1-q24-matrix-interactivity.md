## 2024-03-21 - [Arborist] Feature-Gating GTK Dependencies in Matrix Handler

**Anomaly:** The `matrix` handler inherently linked to `gtk4` and `glib`, forcing UI compilation even for non-GTK targets (like Qt builds), severely breaching the separation of logic and presentation layers. Additionally, expanding nodes in the `MatrixTetra` was rigid.
**Resolution:** Explicitly gated `gtk4` and `glib` inside `Cargo.toml` utilizing `optional = true` alongside a new `[features]` block. Wrapped the respective structural presentation elements (`create_view`, `ProjectView`) utilizing `#[cfg(feature = "gtk")]` inside `lib.rs` to shield the Qt compilation cycle. Engineered an efficient zero-copy, in-place depth-first traversal mutation routine (`toggle_node`) on the `ExpandableList` in `tetra.rs` to dynamically flip `is_expanded` on boolean node IDs.
