## 2024-05-14 - [Matrix Bubble Render Fixes]
**Anomaly:** AppKit `NSTextField` naturally fights to expand to its intrinsic single-line width, pushing the `NSBox` container bounds outward and breaking text wrapping horizontally. Additionally, `NSBoxCustom` can default to being visually transparent, preventing the bubble background and border from rendering properly, particularly causing low contrast against system window backgrounds.
**Resolution:** Shattered intrinsic width resistance on the `NSTextField` by explicitly lowering horizontal compression resistance (250.0). Configured the `NSBox` with explicit line borders and forced `setTransparent: NO` to prevent silent invisibility, using `NSColor::textBackgroundColor()` and `NSColor::separatorColor()` for optical separation across themes.
