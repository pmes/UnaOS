## 2024-05-18 - [Lightweight Context Translator]
**Anomaly:** Raw `DICTIONARY$TOPOLOGY` IPC payloads consume excessive LLM tokens and cause pointer-dereferencing hallucinations due to non-semantic formatting.
**Resolution:** Implemented `decompress_topology` to parse the `$`/`|`/`:` delimiters into a highly semantic `Node relies on: Dependency1, Dependency2` format. Designed to fail gracefully without thread panics.
